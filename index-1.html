StudyBuddy
AI Homework Helper

A patient, always-awake study partner. Ask it a question — math, science, English, history — and it walks through the reasoning step by step, instead of just handing you a final answer to copy.

Explained clearly in 3 steps, on average.

features

What it actually helps with

🧮 Step-by-step solving

Math, physics, and chemistry problems broken into the reasoning that leads to the answer.

✍️ Essay feedback

Grammar and structure notes explained in plain language, not just red underlines.

🗂️ Subject organizer

Tracks homework by class and due date so nothing slips through.

💬 Follow-up questions

Ask "why?" as many times as you need — the explanation doesn't end at the first answer.

🌙 Dark mode

Built for the 9 PM homework session, not just the daytime one.

📊 Progress tracker

Shows which topics you've practiced most, so gaps are easy to spot.

Installation

  1. Clone the repository: 
    git clone https://github.com/yourusername/studybuddy.git
  2. Move into the project folder: 
    cd studybuddy
  3. Open index.html in your browser — no build step required.
  4. (Optional) Add your API key to config.js to connect a live AI backend.
how it works

Usage

  1. Open the app and choose a subject — Math, Science, English, and more.
  2. Type or paste your homework question into the input box.
  3. Click Get Help and read the step-by-step explanation.
  4. Ask a follow-up question if any step is unclear.
  5. Mark the question "understood" to track progress over time.

FAQ

Does StudyBuddy just give me the answer to copy?

No — it walks through the reasoning first. The final answer comes last, after the steps that lead to it.

What subjects are supported?

Math, Physics, Chemistry, English, and History at a high-school level, with more subjects planned.

Is it free to use?

Yes, the core homework-help features are free. A premium tier with unlimited daily questions is planned.

Does it work offline?

No, an internet connection is required since explanations are generated by the AI engine in real time.
